Options Ideas BiWeekly

30 Sep 2018 on investing

Some tips:

  • When vols are low, buy options. When vols are high, sell options.
  • When underlying is high and doesn’t look likely to go any higher, sell protected calls at above current price. Pocket the premiums.
  • When underlying is low and doesn’t look likely to go any lower, sell puts at below current price. Pocket the premiums.
