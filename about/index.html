Patrick Leung

These are the working notes of a journeyman programmer in the financial industry. A veteran of many big bank projects in Wall Street, Patrick has built trading engines in C++, risk analytics in C#, portfolio risk viewers in Java, and electronic trading systems in C++. Currently residing in Singapore, Patrick enjoys fiddling with F#, Python, and TypeScript while not in the office. He also enjoys a good game of table tennis and 10km runs around Bedok Resevoir.

Patrick is a graduate of Stuyvesant High School in NYC, the Cooper Union School of Engineering and the Lubin School of Business.
