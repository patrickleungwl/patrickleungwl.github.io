Patrick Leung

These are the working notes of a journeyman programmer in the financial industry. A veteran of many big bank projects in Wall Street, Patrick has built trading engines in C++, risk analytics in C#, and portfolio risk viewers in Java. Currently stationed in Singapore, Patrick enjoys fiddling with R and Python while not in the office. He also enjoys a good game of table tennis and 10km runs around Bedok Resevoir.

Patrick is a graduate of the Cooper Union School of Engineering and the Lubin School of Business in NYC .
